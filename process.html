Our 4-Step Process

We've refined our process over 25 years to ensure every project is executed with precision, transparency, and exceptional attention to detail.

01

Consultation & Design

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and property characteristics. Our designers visit your home to assess the site, discuss your ideas, and provide initial recommendations. We explore design concepts, materials, and features that will bring your vision to life while complementing your home's architecture.

  • Initial site assessment and measurement
  • Comprehensive needs and lifestyle analysis
  • Preliminary design concepts and budget estimation
  • Material and finish selection guidance
02

3D Design & Planning

Our designers create detailed 3D renderings that bring your vision to life before construction begins. Using advanced design software, we develop comprehensive plans including structural engineering, plumbing, electrical, and automation systems. This phase ensures every detail is considered and approved before breaking ground.

  • Detailed 3D modeling and virtual reality walkthroughs
  • Comprehensive engineering and technical plans
  • Material and equipment specifications
  • Permitting and regulatory compliance management
03

Construction & Craftsmanship

Master craftsmen execute the design with precision, using premium materials and time-tested techniques. Our project managers oversee every phase of construction, maintaining clear communication and ensuring the highest quality standards. We maintain a clean, organized worksite and respect your property throughout the construction process.

  • Expert excavation and structural work
  • Plumbing, electrical, and equipment installation
  • Premium finishing with attention to detail
  • Regular progress updates and site meetings
04

Delivery & Support

We ensure complete satisfaction with thorough orientation and ongoing support for your new aquatic environment. Our team provides comprehensive training on all systems, maintenance guidance, and a detailed warranty package. We remain available for any questions or support long after the project is complete.

  • Comprehensive system training and orientation
  • Detailed maintenance instructions and schedule
  • Warranty registration and documentation
  • Ongoing support and maintenance programs

Project Timeline

While each project is unique, most luxury pool projects follow a similar timeline from initial consultation to completion.

Weeks 1-4

Design & Planning Phase

Initial consultation, site assessment, conceptual design, 3D modeling, engineering plans, and permitting.

Weeks 5-12

Construction Phase

Excavation, structural work, plumbing, electrical, equipment installation, and interior finishing.

Week 13

Filling & Startup

Final cleaning, filling the pool, system startup, water chemistry balancing, and final inspections.

Week 14

Orientation & Completion

Client orientation, system training, final walkthrough, and project completion documentation.
