Our Story

Founded in 1998 by John and Michael Advent, Advent Pools began with a simple vision: to transform Houston backyards into breathtaking aquatic environments that elevate the art of outdoor living. What started as a small family business has grown into Houston's premier luxury pool design and construction company.

Over the past 25 years, we have crafted more than 150 custom pools for discerning homeowners across Houston's most prestigious neighborhoods. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation as the region's most trusted luxury pool builder.

Today, led by second-generation owner David Advent, we continue to push the boundaries of aquatic design while maintaining the craftsmanship and attention to detail that has defined our work from the beginning.
