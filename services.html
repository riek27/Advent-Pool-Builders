Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of luxury pool design and construction services, creating bespoke aquatic environments tailored to your vision and lifestyle.

Custom Luxury Pools

Our signature service, custom luxury pools are designed from the ground up to reflect your personal style, complement your home's architecture, and enhance your outdoor living experience. We work with you to create a unique aquatic environment that becomes the centerpiece of your property.

  • Complete custom design from concept to completion
  • Premium materials including natural stone, glass tile, and exotic hardwoods
  • Advanced lighting and automation systems
  • Integration with existing architecture and landscape
  • 3D modeling and virtual reality walkthroughs

Outdoor Living Spaces

Extend your living area beyond your home with a complete outdoor environment designed for entertainment, relaxation, and enjoyment. We create cohesive spaces that seamlessly integrate pools with outdoor kitchens, dining areas, fire features, and landscaping.

  • Custom outdoor kitchens with premium appliances
  • Fire pits, fireplaces, and fire tables
  • Entertainment areas with audio/visual systems
  • Landscape design and hardscaping

Water & Fire Features

Create captivating visual and sensory experiences with custom water and fire features. From elegant waterfalls to dramatic fire bowls, these elements add movement, sound, and warmth to your outdoor environment, creating an atmosphere of luxury and tranquility.

  • Custom waterfalls, fountains, and water walls
  • Fire bowls, fire pits, and linear fire features
  • Interactive water features for children
  • Rain curtain and laminar flow features
  • Integrated water and fire combinations

Pool Remodeling & Renovation

Breathe new life into your existing pool with our comprehensive remodeling services. We transform outdated pools into modern masterpieces, updating finishes, adding new features, and improving functionality while preserving the pool's structural integrity.

  • Complete pool resurfacing with modern finishes
  • Tile and coping replacement
  • Equipment upgrades for efficiency and performance
  • Addition of new features (spas, waterfalls, lighting)
  • Deck and surround renovations
